U.S. citizens traveling to India require a valid passport with at least six months validity and two blank pages, an Indian visa or OCI card, and optionally an e-arrival form. Most travelers can use an e-Visa for tourism or business, applied for online at the Indian Visa Online website. A valid, pre-approved visa is required to board your flight.

Essential Travel Documents Checklist
Passport: Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond the date of entry and have at least 2 blank pages.
Visa/OCI Card:
e-Visa: For short-term, tourist, or business visits (up to 60 or 180 days, depending on type). Apply directly via the Indian government website.
Regular Visa: Required for longer stays or specific purposes (work, student), processed through VFS Global.
OCI Card: For Overseas Citizens of India, this replaces the visa requirement.
E-Arrival Form (Optional): Starting Oct 2025, travelers can submit an electronic arrival (disembarkation) form online up to 72 hours before travel, though paper forms are still accepted until March 2026.
Vaccination Certificate: Yellow Fever vaccination is required only if arriving from an infected area.
Proof of Travel: Return/onward ticket is typically required.
